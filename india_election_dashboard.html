Structural Summary: Key Correlations
// VALIDATED FINDINGS FROM 10,572 CONSTITUENCY-ELECTIONS
Electorate Size Effect
Every 1 Lakh additional voters → ~5% turnout drop. R² correlation confirmed across 10K+ observations.
Candidate Fragmentation
Contests with 20+ candidates average 63% turnout vs 81% for 1–5 candidates. Each additional 5 candidates = ~4.5% drop.
ST Reservation Advantage
ST constituencies average 77.7% vs 71.2% GEN. Community mobilization and strong local identity are likely drivers.
Urban-Rural Divide
Urban constituencies in Maharashtra, Delhi, Bihar show consistently lower turnout (40–55%) vs rural equivalents (70–85%).
